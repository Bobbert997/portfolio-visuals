Six-Pillar Execution Framework

1

Foundation · First

Indexing & De-Indexing Control

Daily GSC workflows. Index priority pages, remove obsolete and parameterised URLs. Clean crawl budget before anything else.

2

Multiplier · Second

Site Structure & Topical Silos

Define silos, flatten URL depth to ≤3 levels, align navigation, breadcrumbs, and internal links to silo hierarchy.

3

Clarity · Third

Keyword Mapping & Cannibalisation

One primary keyword per canonical page per silo. Merge or redirect competing pages within and across silos.

4

Consolidation · Fourth

Redirect & Canonical Strategy

301 all legacy URLs into correct silo pages. Remove redirect chains. Canonicalise filter and parameter variants to silo entry points.

5

Alignment · Fifth

Content Reframing

Rewrite category intros for silo context. Improve H1–H3 structure. Strengthen intra-silo internal linking throughout.

6

Expansion · Last

Content Refresh & Net-New Pages

Expand only after structure is stable. No new pages unless they strengthen an existing silo. No exceptions.

↑ Pillars executed in strict priority order — sequence is non-negotiable.
